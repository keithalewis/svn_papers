One Period Model

The one period model assumes m instruments are available to be traded at the beginning of a period and trading halts at the end of the period. No trading is permitted during the period. Let x\in R^m denote the prices of of the intruments at the beginning of the period and X\colon\Omega\to R^m be the prices at the end of the period.
