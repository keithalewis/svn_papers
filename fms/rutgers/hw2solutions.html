Exercise 1. In the one period model described in the FTAP reading, show \(\Xi_0\cdot X_0 = E[\sum_{j=1}^n A_j \Pi_j]\), where \(A_j = \Xi_{j-1}\cdot(X_j + C_j) - \Xi_j\cdot X_j\), \(0\le j\le n\) and \(\Xi_{-1} = 0 = \Xi_n\).

We have \begin{aligned} E\sum_{j=1}^n A_j \Pi_j &= E\sum_{j=1}^n (\Xi_{j-1}\cdot(X_j + C_j) - \Xi_j\cdot X_j) \Pi_j \\ &= E\sum_{j=0}^{n-1} \Xi_{j}\cdot(X_{j+1} + C_{j+1}) \Pi_{j+1} - \sum_{j=1}^n \Xi_j\cdot X_j \Pi_j \\ &= E\sum_{j=0}^{n-1} E_j[\Xi_{j}\cdot(X_{j+1} + C_{j+1}) \Pi_{j+1}] - \sum_{j=1}^n \Xi_j\cdot X_j \Pi_j \\ &= E\sum_{j=0}^{n-1} \Xi_{j}\cdot X_j \Pi_j - \sum_{j=1}^n \Xi_j\cdot X_j \Pi_j \\ &= \Xi_0\cdot X_0 - E[\Xi_n\cdot X_n\Pi n] \\ &= \Xi_0\cdot X_0. \\ \end{aligned}
