The Ho-Lee model with short rate process \(r_t = \phi(t) + \sigma(t)B_t\) has the closed form solution \[ D(t, u) = e^{-\int_t^u \{\phi(s) - \frac{1}{2}(\Sigma(s) - \Sigma(u))^2\}\,ds - (\Sigma(u) - \Sigma(t))B_t} \] for the price at time \(t\) of a zero coupon bond maturing at time \(u\), where \(\Sigma(t) = \int_0^t \sigma(s)\,ds\).

Exercise 1. Show \(E[D(u,v)D_t] = E[D(u,v)e^\gamma]D(0,t)\) where \(\gamma = (\Sigma(v) - \Sigma(u))\int_0^t \min\{s,u\}\sigma(s)\,ds\).

By Baby Girsanov we have \(\gamma = \Cov(\log D(u,v),\log D_t = \Cov((\Sigma(u) - \Sigma(t))B_t, \int_0^t \sigma(s)B_s\,ds) = (\Sigma(v) - \Sigma(u))\int_0^t \sigma(s)\Cov(B_t, B_s)\,ds\).
