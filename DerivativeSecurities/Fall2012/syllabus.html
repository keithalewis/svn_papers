Equity Derivatives

Syllabus

Description

How to interpret financial problems mathematically and use software to price, hedge, and manage the risk of uncertain cash flows.

Grading

50% of your grade will be determined by weekly homework and the remaining 50% will be based on a final examination.

Definition of derivative security. Introduction to basic derivative types.

Reading: Powerpoint slides, Derivative Securities, Hull chapter 1, Merton section 2. Buffet on derivatives.

Introduction to the xll library. The one period model and examples.

Reading: One Period Model, Grassmann's Calculus (Draft).

Lesson 3

Multiperiod models and examples.

Reading: The Fundamental Theorem of Asset Pricing
Software: ExcelMonte

Lesson 4

Random Walk, algebras of sets as partial information, the multi-period model, Brownian motion.

Reading: Oksendal chapter 3, FTAP.

Reading: Hull chapters 11 and 12. The Pricing of Options and Corporate Liabilities bms.pdf

(Semi) static hedging of barrier options. Exotic derivatives.
